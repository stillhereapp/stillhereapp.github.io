StillHere

Personal Safety Check-In & Emergency Alert App

About

StillHere is a mobile application designed to help people stay safe when they are alone or in potentially risky situations such as late-night walks, travel, or meeting new people.

Users set a check-in timer. If they fail to respond, the app automatically sends an alert message to their chosen emergency contacts.

How SMS Is Used

  • Safety alerts only
  • Sent only to contacts explicitly added by the user
  • No marketing or promotional messages
  • No spam or unsolicited communication
  • Triggered only during emergency or missed check-in events

Technology

StillHere uses Twilio’s SMS API to deliver time-sensitive safety notifications reliably to user-designated contacts.
