Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

About StillHere

StillHere is a personal safety check-in mobile application that allows users to schedule safety reminders and notify emergency contacts if a check-in is missed.

Information We Collect

  • Name (optional)
  • Phone number
  • Emergency contact numbers
  • App usage data related to safety check-ins

How We Use Information

We use your information only to:

  • Send safety reminders
  • Send emergency alerts
  • Provide account notifications
  • Operate and improve the StillHere service

SMS Communications

By registering your phone number and checking the SMS consent checkbox during account registration, you consent to receive SMS messages from StillHere related to safety check-ins and emergency alerts. These messages are strictly transactional and not promotional.

Message frequency varies based on your check-in schedule. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

You may opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us at admin@stillhere.dev.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information.
