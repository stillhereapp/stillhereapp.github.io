Privacy Policy

About StillHere

StillHere is a personal safety check-in mobile application that allows users to schedule safety reminders and notify emergency contacts if a check-in is missed.

Information We Collect

  • Name (optional)
  • Phone number
  • Emergency contact numbers
  • App usage data related to safety check-ins

How We Use Information

We use your information only to:

  • Send safety reminders
  • Send emergency alerts
  • Provide account notifications
  • Operate and improve the StillHere service

SMS Communications

By registering your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to safety check-ins and emergency alerts. These messages are strictly transactional and not promotional.

You may opt-out anytime by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information.
